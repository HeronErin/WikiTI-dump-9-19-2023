Welcome to WikiTI, a Wiki for programmers and hardware hackers of Texas Instruments's graphing calculators. WikiTI's goal is to serve as a central place for the discussion and exploration of the undocumented features of the TI-OS and hardware. To browse our documentation, you can click the "Calculator Documentation" link to the left.

Any programmer or hacker is free to contribute any documentation of the TI-OS, even if the information is incomplete or possibly wrong. For more information, see our page on Contributing. Not sure where to start contributing? Check out the list of Wanted Pages that are linked to but don't yet exist, the list of stub pages that need filled out, the list of pages with missing information, and the list of pages that need to be formatted.

Contacting the WikiTI Administration

If you wish to contact the maintainers of WikiTI, for any reason, on IRC, please join #ti on EFnet and ask for DrDnar or BrandonW. If you do not have an IRC client, use this. You can find email addresses and IM names on each administrator's User page (soon), or just leave a message on their Talk page.
